Accessibility Standards & Engineering Best Practices

What is this for?

This guide defines the mandatory engineering standards for all Salesforce vendors. Use these checklists to validate WCAG 2.1 compliance during design and development phases, prior to final QA handoff.

  • Screen Reader: Test critical flows with Safari + VoiceOver (Mac) or NVDA (Windows).
  • Zoom: Verify layout at 200% browser zoom.
